Game of Life

Technologies: JS, React, lodash

Create a pattern, watch life generate.

Conway's game of life is based off a couple of simple rules. If you’re alive and have two or three neighbors you’ll stay live. If you’re dead and have three neighbors you’re alive. Every other scenario you're dead. With these simple rules generations can make some pretty interesting patterns. Users can also toggle pixel size and generation speed.
